## The plain version

Alien Motion Tracker is a local app. No data are sent over internet.  

The app uses your phone's movement sensors for the gameplay, so player can rotate their phone to track the virtual motion.  

Remote control is a proximity local control for in-person gameplay It uses WiFi connection to connect both phone for convenience,  so one "admin" phone can control what a "player" tracker phone sees during an in-person gameplay session.   

But it never communicate over internet. It just use the local wifi, the play information stays with the phones at your table  

I do not receive, access, or use personal data from the app. I will never share app data with anyone, including anonymous data.

**Last updated:** June 13, 2026  
**Developer:** Ludic RPG  
**Privacy contact:** [privacy@ludicrpg.com](mailto:privacy@ludicrpg.com)

## What the app does

**Phone movement.**  
The app reads how the phone is turned and moved. That is what makes the tracker react when a player sweeps the phone around the table. It uses motion sensors only, no location service like GPS.

This is used live by the app. Never recorded and never transmitted over internet to any party.

**Your home Wi-Fi network.**  
The app checks whether the phone are connected over Wi-Fi. It just get the status of the connection not even the name of your WiFi (No SSID access). This connection is local between the phone and your home WiFi router. It never uses internet.

Since no personal data is transfered between the phones, it is safe to use on public WiFi network.

## What data the app uses

The app does not access any personal information like your name, email address, phone number, contacts, photos, videos, microphone, camera, or location.

**Stored on your device.**  
The app stores a random app ID on your phone so the Admin phone can recognize the same player tracker again if it reconnects. 

It may also remember the last local connection address, your selected tracker theme, and the Admin's current session state (targets positions, trackers status...)

This is local app data. Some of it is shared with the other phones in the same active table session because that is how the prop works.

**Purchases.**  
Apple or Google handles the payment. I do not receive your payment card number.

## Sharing and tracking

I do not sell app data. I do not share app data with advertisers or data brokers, even anonymous data. I do not use the app to follow you across other apps or websites.

## When you contact me

If you email me, message me on Discord: I receive whatever you choose to send. That might include your email address, username, message, screenshots, phone model, operating system version, or bug details.

I use that only to answer you, understand feedback, fix bugs, and support the app.

## Retention and deletion

**Local app data.**  
Delete the app to delete its local data from your phone. On Android, you can also clear the app storage from system settings. Ending an Admin session deletes the saved session state for that session.

**Support messages.**  
If you contact me directly, I may keep the conversation while it is useful for support, bug fixing, project history, or legal reasons. You can ask me to delete direct support messages I control by emailing [privacy@ludicrpg.com](mailto:privacy@ludicrpg.com).

**No account deletion flow.**  
The app does not create Ludic RPG accounts, so there is no app account to delete.

## Changes

I may update this policy when the app changes or when store requirements change. The date at the top tells you when it was last revised.

If the app ever starts collecting data from the app itself, I will not hide it in tiny legal fog. This page will say what changed and why.
